How It Works

Subscribe → Hold → Redeem

01

DAO Issues Obligations

The DAO holds T-Bills as underlying assets, then issues synthetic obligations onchain. You get exposure to T-Bill yields, not the T-Bills themselves.

02

You Subscribe (KYC Required)

Complete KYC/AML verification, then subscribe anytime before maturity. The earlier you enter, the more yield you earn. Priced fairly based on time remaining.

03

Yield Accrues

Every day that passes, the value grows toward par. No staking, no claiming. Just automatic accrual tracked by the protocol.

04

Repo for Liquidity

Need cash now? Lock your position and get instant liquidity from verified lenders. They earn your yield while you're in the repo. Repay anytime.

05

Redeem at Maturity

When obligations mature, verified users redeem at face value in USDC. The DAO settles from T-Bill proceeds. You never touch the actual T-Bills.

Under The Hood

Smart Contract Stack

Modular design. Each contract does one thing well. No god contracts, no upgrade drama.

Series Registry
Tracks all T-Bill series with CUSIP numbers, maturity dates, and proof of custody
Yield Engine
Pure math. Calculates your yield based on time held. No external data feeds required
Token Manager
Handles your shares, transfers, and makes sure yield follows the tokens correctly
Repo Escrow
Locks collateral, matches lenders, and transfers yield rights during repo periods
Treasury Vault
Holds USDC backing and processes redemptions when T-Bills mature
